Community Store

The Community Store allows users to explore project outputs, understand how they were used in real contexts, and identify resources that may be relevant to their own activities.

Rural–Urban Enablers (RUEs)

A structured catalogue of tools and services addressing concrete rural–urban challenges. Each RUE page explains:

  • What the tool or service does
  • Who it is intended for
  • How it was used within the project
  • What type of outputs it produces

RUEs can be browsed by thematic domain and territorial applicability.

Explore RUEs

Immersive Campaigns

A collection of immersive content developed through rural–urban co-creation activities.

This section allows users to explore immersive material directly through a web browser, without the need for specialised hardware, in order to understand the context, narratives, and experiences developed during the project.

View Campaigns

Knowledge Inventory

A curated repository of project knowledge, including:

  • Good practices from Rural–Urban Co-Creation Labs (RUCLs)
  • Practice abstracts and business models
  • Policy briefs and synthesis documents

Content is organised to support learning, reuse, and comparison across different rural–urban contexts.

Browse Inventory

Community of Practice Suite

An integrated entry point to participatory processes and stakeholder interaction tools, based on the Decidim platform.

This suite supports dialogue, engagement, and collective reflection among rural and urban actors involved in rural–urban development processes.

Access and Availability

The Community Store is currently available in English. Additional languages are provided where content has been developed and used in specific RUCL localities.

Access policies and the level of integration vary by section and by tool, depending on maturity and usage context.
